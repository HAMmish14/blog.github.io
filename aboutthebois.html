ABOUT
THE BOIS

The team that designed this page





Welcome to our blog! We are Santhosh, Meshach, and Bageesh, three passionate Indian boys who share a common love for video games, writing, and adventure. Our journey began in Bangalore, a vibrant city known for its technology, culture, and the perfect blend of tradition and modernity.

Individually, we each discovered our own unique interests and talents, but it was our shared enthusiasm for gaming that brought us together. We would often spend countless hours competing against each other, exploring virtual worlds, and discussing the latest releases. However, we soon realized that our passion for gaming extended beyond mere entertainment.


Driven by our curiosity and eagerness to share our experiences, we started writing articles about our favorite games, analyzing their mechanics, discussing their impact on society, and delving into the cultural aspects surrounding them. Our articles resonated with like-minded individuals, sparking conversations and establishing a small community of gamers who valued thoughtful insights and engaging discussions.

As we continued our exploration of the gaming world, we also developed a strong affinity for travel. Bangalore, with its surrounding landscapes and vibrant city life, offered us the perfect playground for our adventures. We would embark on long bike and car trips, exploring the hidden gems of the city, discovering new cafes, capturing breathtaking views, and immersing ourselves in the local culture.


It was during one of these exhilarating trips that we had an epiphany. Why not combine our passions for gaming, writing, and travel into a cohesive project? And that's how our blog was born. With the aim of creating a platform that celebrates gaming, encourages intellectual discussions, and showcases the beauty of Bangalore, we embarked on this project as part of our college curriculum.


Our blog is a space where we share our gaming experiences, discuss the latest trends, review new releases, and provide insightful analysis. We also document our travel escapades, sharing tips, itineraries, and hidden gems we uncover during our explorations. Through our articles, we hope to inspire fellow gamers, provoke thoughtful conversations, and encourage everyone to embark on their own adventures.


Join us as we navigate the virtual worlds, pen our thoughts, and venture out into the vibrant streets of Bangalore. We are excited to have you on this journey with us, and we hope that our blog will provide you with valuable insights, entertainment, and inspiration. Together, let's celebrate the joy of gaming, the power of words, and the beauty of exploration.
