Best Places to Eat in Bangalore


Bangalore, the bustling city known for its vibrant culture and diverse culinary scene, is a food lover's paradise. With a plethora of options ranging from traditional South Indian delicacies to global cuisines, the city offers a remarkable dining experience. Here are some of the best places to eat in Bangalore that you simply cannot miss.


  1. Mavalli Tiffin Room (MTR): A legendary establishment known for its authentic South Indian breakfast, MTR is a must-visit. Indulge in their crispy dosas, fluffy idlis, and aromatic filter coffee for a truly satisfying start to the day.

  2. Koshy's: With its old-world charm and delectable European fare, Koshy's has been a favorite haunt for Bangaloreans for decades. From their mouthwatering steaks to their signature desserts, every dish here is a delight.

  3. Vidyarthi Bhavan: Step into this iconic eatery and transport yourself back in time. Known for its scrumptious masala dosas, Vidyarthi Bhavan is a haven for those seeking an authentic taste of Karnataka.

  4. Nagarjuna: If you're a fan of Andhra cuisine, Nagarjuna is the place to be. Their fiery biryanis, spicy curries, and delectable seafood will leave you craving for more.

  5. Taaza Thindi: A hidden gem in Bangalore, Taaza Thindi offers a unique street food experience. From the famous "Thatte Idli" to mouthwatering chaats and delicious desserts, this place is a paradise for food enthusiasts.

  6. Social: Combining good food with a trendy ambiance, Social is a popular hangout spot. Indulge in their innovative dishes, sip on refreshing cocktails, and soak in the lively atmosphere.

  7. Toit: Beer lovers shouldn't miss Toit, a microbrewery known for its handcrafted brews and delicious pub grub. With a wide range of flavors and a cozy setting, it's the perfect place to unwind with friends.

  8. Brahmin's Coffee Bar: This humble establishment is famous for its filter coffee and crispy, golden-brown medu vadas. Start your day with a hearty breakfast here, and you'll be fueled up for a day of exploring.

  9. Empire Restaurant: For a taste of Bangalore's vibrant street food scene, head to Empire. From juicy kebabs to flavorful biryanis, their menu is a treat for meat lovers.

  10. Caperberry: If you're looking for an upscale dining experience, Caperberry offers a fusion of Mediterranean and European cuisines. Their exquisite dishes and elegant presentation make for a memorable gastronomic adventure.

Whether you're a local or a visitor, Bangalore's culinary landscape offers something for every palate. From traditional flavors to international delights, these top dining destinations will leave you with a memorable and satisfying experience.
